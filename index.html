Abstract of the Camp

WIDE Members,

Without any doubt, the core competence of WIDE Project is WIDE member. What every WIDE member is currently doing, must be the most active and interesting work. In past, we did a lot of work together, which usually ignited at the WIDE Camp by sharing interest or directions, followed by fruitful discussions.

This time, we would like to use the best part of the WIDE camp -- good amount of time for endless late night discussions -- to ignite new activities based on current on-going work of each member. Of course, we will have regular BoF and research presentations.

Our topics may include following topics. Please provide *YOUR* session ideas to PC member either on the registration and/or the questionnaire. (multiple submission is welcome).

Root DNS、RPKI, Routing, Smart City, BGP Security, IT and disaster, Software Defined Media, Network and game, Edge Heavy, Stream Oriented Data Processing, Software Packet I/O, Digital Identity, Cloud, W3C/HTML5/JavaScript, Open Data, Quantum Network

If you haven't answered questionnaire, this is the link.
good time to answer! You don't need to fill same answer in both of forms.

Please come to join us.

Shigeya Suzuki
Chair of WIDE Camp March 2015,
Keio University
and, all 1503 pc members.
